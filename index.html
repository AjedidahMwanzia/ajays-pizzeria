About us

A kenyan Kamba girl, inspired by the pure love of food, joy and her mother's own delicious cooking and love, decides to pursue her love for good food and opens a pizza place .This restaurant is in honour of my african mother and all other queens who go out of their way to make us delicious meals .At ajays pizzeria we don't just sell pizza, we share in the joy and love of an african mother,we are a family.Join us and become part of the family .We are located at Utawala Capital shopping Mall mavoko sub-county machakos county.Ajays pizzeria we are your family
